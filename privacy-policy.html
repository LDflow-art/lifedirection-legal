Privacy Policy

App: Life Direction
Developer: LD Focus
Last updated: 20 March 2026

1. Overview

Life Direction is a personal growth and daily reflection app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Life Direction does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no user accounts, no login, no backend server, and no internet connection requirement.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All data you enter — including your name, age, focus areas, tasks, and journal entries — is stored locally on your device only using Android DataStore. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

4. No Third-Party Services

Life Direction does not integrate any analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. No third-party SDKs that collect data are included in this app.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by using the logout option within the app, or by uninstalling the app from your device. Both actions permanently remove all locally stored data.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the updated version will be published at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our Ko-fi page at https://ko-fi.com/ld_focus.
